ABOUT

  • Drained water pipe position: You should provide fitting of pipes for drained water in the northeast. The bathroom floor slope should be towards north and east, so water drains to the northeast side of the bathroom.
  • Shower, taps, and mirror position: Shower and fixtures in the bathroom can be attached to the northern wall.
  • Geyser position: You can place the geyser in the southeast corner.
  • Bathtub & Washbasin position: You can place the bathtub in the western corner and provide space for a washbasin in the northeastern corner.
  • Overhead tank position: The Overhead tanks should be in the southwest part of the site.
  • Window/ ventilator position: There should be windows or ventilators in the east or the north.
  • Showerhead/ bath position: You can take a bath in the western position of bathroom
  • Bathrooms without W.C./ commode: You can place bathrooms without attached Toilet in the east or between east and north-east of your home.
  • W.C./ Commode position and orientation: If there is an attached toilet in the bathroom itself, W.C. should be on the West, South, or Northwest side and a few inches above the ground. W.C. should always be on the north-south axis and never on the east-west axis.
  • Color scheme: It is advisable to choose bright and soothing colors for bathroom walls. Ideal Colours: White, Grey or Pink
  • Mirror position: You can place the mirror on the eastern and northern walls of your bathroom.
  • Dirty linens position: It would help if you placed the dirty linens on the west side of the bathroom as setting rays are very harmful to bacteria and kill them.
  • Cabinet position: If there is a cabinet, it should always be on the southwest side of the bathroom + Toilet.
  • Toilet position: Keep Toilet locations at a safe distance from the kitchen and the dining room.
  • Door position: Place the door of the Toilet on the eastern side.
  • Septic Tank position: Avoid building a septic tank on the Southern side.
  • Washing machine position: The appropriate directions for keeping the washing machine are southeast and northwest.
